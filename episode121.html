I established 29CM's design guide for online advertising. 29CM has many different categories and numerous brands. In these countless situations, my goal was to help each brand contents designer to design more efficiently under a certain guide.
Front
29CM's online advertising did not have a design guide. Depending on the designers and the situations, it was designed. It causes long production times that are difficult to schedule and consistently low conversion rates. Recognizing the problem, I decided to provide an optimal design guide for 29CM's online advertising. I took on the role of the project owner and brand content designer and carried out the project as an independent TF team.
