Product Designer. Facilitator.

Jay Youngjun Kim studied graphic design at Yonsei University from 2003 to 2010. He continued studies in Seoul, where he attended the postgraduate course in graphic design at Kookmin University. He graduated with a final major project regarding Font Round-title, and a thesis titled 'Rediscovering the value of font Gulim and developing as use of a title font' in 2012.

In the meantime, he worked as an interaction designer at Ogilvy Korea from 2011 to 2012. At that time, he belonged to a digital studio that worked with various clients such as IBM, Dove, Qualcomm and so on. Afterward, he worked as an AD graphic designer(freelancer) of KT OllehTV at OMS Product Team, KT Skylife.

Then he crossed to London, and he finished the MA Interaction Design Communication program with a thesis called 'For points of compromise between privacy and surveillance, a proposition of a rational system design for surveillance drone policing' in 2016 at London College of Communication, University of the Arts London. Thereafter, he worked as a product designer at FINDA, a financial recommendation service from 2017 to 2018, and worked as a design lead at AITRICS focusing on data-driven health intelligence from 2018 to 2019. At COINONE, a cryptocurrency trading platform, he is currently contributing to all facets of design to create visually engaging and innovative products, while bringing his vision and concepts to life.
