DESIGENE
DESIGENE typeface aims at the possibility of 'Variability' and 'Flexibility' of typefaces. Most fonts have been traditionally made as static forms which have been finished and published. It means that users normally download and install complete fonts from designers who made those typefaces. There are no possibilities of typefaces being able to be altered themselves without the abilities of each user. In this respect, a genetically modified typeface is made based on a form of the motif 'DNA' as a primary sample. From the top to the bottom, a letter or characters are randomly and automatically designed with variously sized particles in real time when people download and install the font 'DESIGENE'.

DESIGENE typeface is programmed by Nodebox based on Helvetica Bold and YoonGotic Bold. With the shape and pattern of this system, the font can be changed to anything.
