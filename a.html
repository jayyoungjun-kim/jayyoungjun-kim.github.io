GENETIC TYPEFACE 'A'
The aim of this project is for an experiment of distinctive geometric shapes of typefaces automatically composed of lines through computational analysis. These typefaces embody a genetic mid-process in terms of the imagery of creation of organism from nothing to a complete form as a typeface. The typefaces randomly consist of bilateral symmetric and asymmetric lines utilised a computational system. In addition, the alphabet ‘A’ is a typical form having countless possibilities to transform compared to other alphabets.

Using Open Source 'Sketchy Structures’ made by Jay Weeks on Scriptographer
