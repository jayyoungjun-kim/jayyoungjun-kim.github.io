Marina Hill Jeju
MARINA HILL JEJU is a townhouse located in Seogwipo-si, Jeju-do. It was built by aiming the southern European styled house in Jeju-do and brand identity was developed as the well-matched atmosphere of the construction.
Symbol Ideation, Draft
Three keywords such as the southern European style, white house, and beach were considered as suitably representative of MARINA HILL JEJU. By designing the symbols based on the keywords, the most important task was each simple expression to combine each element to an iconic symbol. Accordingly, I variously tested the southern European style as a curved or peaked roof, white house as a simple door and wall by using geometrical figures, and beach as a wave with curved lines.
Final Symbol
The final symbol merged each form was made with an integrated sentence of each keyword called 'White house of the southern European style on the beach.
Typeface
SF Old Republic font is a geometric sans serif typeface that is designed by ShyFonts. The form of the font is minimal without any featured shape and wide in balance. It means that the font is a well-matched with the geometric symbol.
