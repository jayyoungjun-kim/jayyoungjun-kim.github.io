Pixelated World
It is a pixelated color recognition system in real-time. The pixel consists of five sizes related to the brightness of the surrounding. If the surrounding becomes more bright, the dimension of pixels are smaller simultaneously, and if it becomes darker, they are bigger. It means that if the system measures the surrounding at dark place, the pixels are getting bigger. By establishing the system, people can easily catch the range of the colors around them with the change of the pixel's size. Besides, people can experience a verity of combinations of colors each second randomly. It makes our world diverse as well as simplified at the same time.
