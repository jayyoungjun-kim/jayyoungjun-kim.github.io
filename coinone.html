Coinone Home Screen Renewal
Coinone product team proceeded home screen renewal. We focused on the purpose and usability of the exchange to provide the best environment for users. Our key mission was to increase the entry rate of the exchange through the home screen.
Poor exchange conversion rate and banner click-through rate
Previously, the home screen had poor exchange conversion rates and banner click-through rates, despite being an important passage to the exchange.
Provide various and in-depth cryptocurrency information
The home screen had poor exchange conversion rates and banner CTRs, despite being an important passage to the exchange.
People tend to use the market book for monitoring cryptocurrency
Although the conversion rate to go to exchange by using the market book is low, more than half of users monitor, of which 70% monitor mainly the cryptocurrency they are interested in.
People are interested in real-time cryptocurrency trends
When checking the overall market, several information are generally most used such as trading volume, skyrocketing, and plummeting. So, we decided to present the above information at the forefront.
Provide a variety of information on the home screen that could be helpful in trading
By providing various and useful information that can be helpful in trading, it aims to increase the CTR that allows users to convert to various contents as well as increase the conversion rate to go to the exchange. Five categories with detailed information are offered.
