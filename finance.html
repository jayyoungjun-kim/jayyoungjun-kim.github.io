Design Finance
Fintech startup 'Finda' held the 2nd Finda Finance Seminar at Wework Euljiro. Under the theme of 'Design Finance', Four financial startups shared their valuable experiences for better financial experiences. I conducted the entire branding planning and design of the seminar and participated as a speaker.
Poster
D + E - S × I ÷ G = N is an implicit representation of 'Design Finance'. Finance basically means the flow of money, and the arithmetic operation is mathematically the basic calculation unit of the funds that make up this finance. When the letters D to G were calculated by the arithmetic operation, the word DESIGN was completed as N came out, and the sentence 'Design Finance' was used as a symbol that can easily be recalled.
