In this interview, we discussed 29CM's products and the working methods of the Product Design Team.

INFCON 2024 컨퍼런스 발표↗
국내 최대 규모의 IT 컨퍼런스인 인프콘에서 29CM 제품 조직을 대표하여 "1CM 차이나는 아이디어로 1M 앞서가는 서비스 만들기"라는 주제로 발표를 진행했습니다. 발표에서는 29CM가 유저에게 흥미를 줄 아이디어를 어떻게 발굴하는지, 이를 성공적으로 서비스화하고 비즈니스 성장에 기여하는지에 대한 대표 사례를 공유했습니다.
At INFCON, the largest IT conference in Korea, I had the honor of representing the 29CM product team and delivered a presentation titled "Creating a Service 1M Ahead with 1CM of Difference in Ideas." During the session, I shared insights into how 29CM identifies ideas that captivate users, successfully launches services, and contributes to business growth. Key examples and representative cases were shared during the session.

I wrote a blog post on the official 29CM blog titled "Building a Team Operations System for Collective Growth." The post introduces the process of creating a team operations system that enables all product design team members to grow together within a cross-functional product organization, where purpose-driven and functional teams collaborate seamlessly.
2024-03-28
연세대학교 디자인예술학부 졸업작품워크샵 특강
연세대학교 디자인예술학부 졸업반을 대상으로 졸업작업 워크샵에서 "기회를 발견하고 아이디어를 실현하다"라는 주제로 특강을 진행했습니다. 강의는 "틈새시장 공략하기," "한계 수용 능력 극복하기," "추천 전쟁"이라는 세 가지 섹션으로 구성되었으며, 29CM가 프로덕트를 만들어가며 고민했던 다양한 아젠다를 공유했습니다.
I delivered a special lecture titled "Discover Possibility, Fulfill Idea" at the graduation project workshop for senior students in Yonsei University's Department of Design and Arts. The lecture was structured into three sections: "Targeting Niche Markets," "Overcoming Capacity Constraints," and "The Recommendation Wars." It shared insights into various agendas that 29CM has addressed while developing its products.
