Postgraduate Show 2016
Poster-sized captions and a brochure were designed for Postgraduate Show 2016 of MA Interaction Design Communication at London College of Communication. As a lead designer in branding team, I developed the visual identity system, and Ben Branagan working at BB Studio designed the captions and brochure.
Visual Identity System
The word ‘Haptic’ stems from the Greek verb 'haptesthai' which means 'to contact' or 'to touch'. In the modern era, most of what we do is to touch or to contact in daily life.

'Haptics' and 'Interaction' have strongly been connected. In lexical semantics, haptics is any form of “interaction” involving touch or contact. In this sense, haptics is a core value as well as one of the valuable approaches of the field of interaction design.

'Haptica' which means an imaginary world represents our exhibition space. When you enter Haptica, our world is offering extraordinary experiences by contacting and touching our view of the world. It is not a single exhibition to show our works. It will show you the world you live, or you will live.
