Search Earthquakes
As an ever-increasing number of natural disasters that we never expect occur, we are exposed to a wide range of safety hazards. I want to be helpful in designing something like the information of danger zones. Earthquake is one of the most fragile parts which we cannot control at all.
Earthquakes in Japan (Zoom-in)
I firstly assembled the data of earthquakes which have accurate magnitude as well as range through USGS. I could find out the figure of earthquakes occurred for a month from 2014-10-21T22:04:16.400Z to 2014-11-20T18:36:01.500Z and over magnitude 2.5+ that is well-arranged, and then designed a map which is composed of carbon colour and red circles that are conspicuous compared to other colours. This map is possible to see everywhere if you want to check a particular area out.
